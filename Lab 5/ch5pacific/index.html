Enjoy Nature in Luxury

Pacific Trails Resort offers a special loging experience on the California North Coast with panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean. Your stay at the Pacific Trails Resort includes a suptuously appointed private yurt and a cooked-to-order breakfast each morning.

  • Unwind in the heated outdoor pool and whirlpool
  • Explore the coast on your own or join our guided tours
  • Relax in our lodging while enjoying complementary appetizers and beverages
  • Savor nightly fine dining with an ocean view
